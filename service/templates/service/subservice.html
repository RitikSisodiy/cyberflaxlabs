Our app development process

Ideation

  • Understanding and analyzing clients app idea.
  • Profiling business requirements like client base, deliverables, functionalities and core purpose.
  • Validating and refining the idea to make it more desirable.
  • Checking feasibility, time frame and estimated cost.
  • Finalizing the milestones.

Wireframing

  • Creating a prototype of the app considering the type of application (Native or Hybrid).
  • Working on basics like features, user interface, look and feel of the app.
  • Understand and analyze the supposed challenges that might occur at the later stage of development.

Design

  • Get wireframes approved and convert ideas and prototype into visuals.
  • UI/UX team mulls over the constraints of developing an awesome app.
  • Checking thoroughly each parameter considering latest trends and future scope.
  • Creating static mock-ups and getting them approved from the client.

Development

  • Development starts simultaneously with the design.
  • Using agile methodology, we regularly get prototypes approved from clients.
  • Manual coding by following the predefined industrial standards.

QA & Testing

  • Accessing and testing the final quality.
  • QA team checks and tests the app at every stage of development.
  • All known and unknown issues are fixed to make the app is free of glitches.
  • We are known to deliver apps with exceptional quality.

Support

  • We go miles to ensure your app is up-to-date.
  • Constant monitoring, support, OS upgrade and optimization so that your app is up and running.
  • Bug fixing, app upgrades, analytic tracking makes us one of the best support and maintenance offering Android app Development Company.
